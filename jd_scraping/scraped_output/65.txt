JobInfrastructure Solutions Program ManagerApply to JobLocation pin iconWarsaw, PolandApply to JobMeta's Enterprise Engineering (EE) department is seeking a highly motivated Infrastructure Solutions Manager with experience in developing strategies and programs to join our Enterprise Infrastructure & Security (EIS) organization. In this role, you’ll be responsible for programs that deliver reliable and innovative infrastructure solutions for services leveraged by the entire company. You’ll be accountable for driving prioritization, program and project development, and net new investment decisions that scale Meta's enterprise. This unique role is embedded closely with multiple engineering teams, where you will be a key driver of piloting new technologies. You’ll be responsible for driving recommendations to executive leadership, ensuring delivery via implementation and cross-functional program engagement, and ensuring that the planning and execution process continuously improves. The candidate will be responsible for maintaining relationships across Meta, owning business unit engagements, and collaborating with both technical and non-technical cross-functional partners.Infrastructure Solutions Program Manager ResponsibilitiesDrive alignment of various verticals within Enterprise Engineering and beyond to achieve organizational-level goalsDevelop new program strategies that focus on driving improvements in efficiency, productivity, reliability, and customer satisfaction.Partner cross-functionally with Financial, Business, Technical, and Operational organizations to drive clear process alignment and go-forward short and long-term strategyWork in a very fast-paced and iterative technical environment with engineering and product leaders, ensuring execution of program goalsUse technical judgment to drive new project evaluations, strategic and technological reviews, challenge proposals, and build consensusProactively identify and analyze complex, long-term, critical infrastructure problems with Enterprise Engineering leaders and stakeholdersBring clarity and transparency to every aspect at each level of the role through clear communications, presentations, identifying metrics and KPI that answer critical questions and provide the right level of dataOwn the communications for both near-term and long-term roadmaps, which includes executive updates, detailed project and program briefs, and status updatesMinimum Qualifications4+ years of experience in technical program management or solutions architecture experienceExperience in enterprise infrastructure and application developmentExperience quickly synthesizing large amounts of information into actionable plansExperience operating autonomously across multiple teams, demonstrated critical thinking, and thought leadershipExperience communicating effectively across business and technical teamsExperience thinking multiple steps ahead and taking proactive steps to address roadblocks, issues, and distractionsExperience leading without expressed authority, influencing decisions and outcomesPreferred QualificationsB.S. in Computer Science or a
