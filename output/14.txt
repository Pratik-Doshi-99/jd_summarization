Join Apple, and help us leave the world better than we found it. Developer experience platform team is looking for an extraordinary Machine Learning engineer to join our team. You will help design and implement our machine learning strategy to take our developer experience platform to the next level and help accelerate app development inside Apple. We will be collaborating and working with multi-functional teams and applying algorithms to large-scale data. DescriptionDescriptionDeveloper Experience Platform team provides frameworks, services and developer tools to internal engineering teams in Apple to accelerate their SDLC. We are working on a Generative AI based developer experience platform leveraging LLMs for internal use and assist our app development team. You will work data scientist, Machine Learning engineers, Software engineers to deliver and end to end AI enabled solution for this platform. You will work with existing and new model evaluate them, fine tune them come up with use cases to solve business problems.
