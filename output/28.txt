The applications we build for experience management are vital in deciding how we capture, analyze, and act upon feedback to drive continuous improvement in our products, services, and internal processes! As part of this team, you will work with a highly talented cross-functional team to create a world-class experience management solution. Join us to do the best work of your life with a welcoming, diverse, and talented group of engineers. Bring passion and dedication to the job and there’s no telling what you could accomplish!DescriptionDescriptionAs a member in our team, you will design and develop server-side code to implement features and functionality for Apple’s Experience Management platform. You will work closely with a team of talented professionals to deliver a highly available and scalable enterprise-level experience management platform. You will also work on analyzing and implementing fixes for production issues. You will provide technical leadership and inputs on application architecture strategies, standards, and technology decisions. In this position, you will play a major role in designing, developing, and implementing our cutting-edge, large-scale Experience Management Platform. The ideal candidate is an expert in Java server-side application development and is passionate about building high-performance applications that provide a great user experience.
